My Diary

Secure personal diary app with Google Sign-In

About My Diary

My Diary is a personal journaling application that allows users to securely store notes, photos, videos, and memories. Users can sign in using their Google account to safely access and manage their private diary entries.

Why Google Sign-In is used

We use Google Sign-In only for authentication purposes to securely identify users. We do not access or store your passwords. Only basic profile information such as your name and email is used.

Features

  • Secure personal diary
  • Media support (images, videos, files)
  • Private and encrypted data
  • Easy login with Google
