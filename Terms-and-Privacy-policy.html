MyDiary Terms & Privacy

MyDiary stores journal content on your device by default. Backup and cloud sync are optional convenience features, not guaranteed storage or recovery services.

Local first

Entries, media, and settings stay on your device unless you choose to back up or sync them.

Cloud is optional

Google Drive sync only starts when you choose to sign in and enable cloud features.

Your responsibility

You should keep your own backups for anything important and verify restores before relying on them.

  • Backup, restore, and sync depend on your device, your storage, your network, and third-party services such as Google Drive and Firebase.
  • Those services and features may fail, be delayed, conflict, duplicate content, or become unavailable without notice.
  • To the extent permitted by applicable law, MyDiary does not guarantee that any entry, attachment, backup, or synced copy can always be preserved, restored, or recovered.
Data may be lost, corrupted, duplicated, delayed, or become unavailable because of device failure, app defects, user actions, sync conflicts, account issues, or third-party outages.

Privacy Policy

  • Your diary content is stored locally on your device by default.
  • If you enable Google Drive sync, MyDiary uses the Google account you choose only for backup, restore, and sync features you start.
  • When you sign in, MyDiary stores limited account and device metadata in Firebase so your Android and Windows devices can be linked and managed.
  • This may include your email address, Firebase account ID, sign-in provider, device ID, device model, platform details, and sync timestamps.
  • If available from your Google account, display name and profile photo may also be stored for account display.
  • MyDiary does not sell your diary data.

Your Control

  • You can stop cloud features at any time by signing out.
  • Removing app data or uninstalling the app removes local content from that device.
  • Exported files and local backups remain under your control and should be stored securely by you.
