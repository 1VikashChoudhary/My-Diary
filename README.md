## Data Collection
My Diary does not collect any personal data. All diary entries are stored 
locally on your device and optionally synced to your own Google Drive account.

## Google Drive Sync
If you enable sync, the app uses Google Drive's appData folder — a private 
hidden folder only accessible by this app. Your data is never visible to anyone else.

## Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party.
